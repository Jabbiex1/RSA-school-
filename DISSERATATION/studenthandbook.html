Welcome Message

Welcome to Royal Science Academy! This handbook serves as your guide to the values, policies, and expectations of our school community.

School Overview

Royal Science Academy is committed to providing a nurturing environment for academic excellence, character development, and innovation.

Academic Policies

  • Regular assignments, homework, and assessments are compulsory.
  • Grades are awarded based on continuous assessment and final exams.
  • Academic dishonesty such as cheating or plagiarism will result in disciplinary action.

Behavior Expectations

  • Respect for teachers, staff, and peers is mandatory.
  • Students must act with honesty, kindness, and integrity at all times.
  • No bullying, harassment, or discrimination is tolerated.

Attendance Guidelines

  • Minimum of 85% attendance is required each term.
  • Late arrivals must report to the front office before joining class.
  • Absences must be justified with a valid note from a parent/guardian.

Disciplinary Measures

Violations of school rules may result in warnings, detention, suspension, or expulsion depending on the severity of the infraction.

Extracurricular Activities

Students are encouraged to participate in sports, clubs, music, debate, and community service for holistic development.

Health & Safety Guidelines

  • Emergency drills are conducted regularly.
  • Students must report illness or injury to a teacher or school nurse immediately.
  • First aid and health services are available on campus.
