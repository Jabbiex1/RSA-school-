School History & Philosophy

Founded in Kaffu Bullom Chiefdom, Lungi, Royal Science Academy began with a vision to provide transformative, science-oriented education from Kindergarten to Junior Secondary School. The School was founded in September 2024. Our philosophy centers on nurturing critical thinking, creativity, and leadership in a safe and inclusive environment.

Accreditation & Affiliations

Royal Science Academy is accredited by the Ministry of Basic and Senior Secondary Education (MBSSE) and maintains affiliations with national and international education bodies focused on STEM development and holistic education practices.

School Leadership

Proprietor 1 & 2

Founders of Royal Science Academy, guiding the overall vision and strategic direction of the school.

Board Chairman

Oversees governance, ensures adherence to educational standards and policies, and supports school leadership.

Principal (JSS Level)

Leads the Junior Secondary School division, focusing on curriculum implementation and student development.

Vice Principal (JSS Level)

Assists the Principal in administrative duties and supports both students and teaching staff.

Headteacher (Primary Level)

Responsible for managing the Primary School, ensuring quality teaching and holistic student growth.

Deputy Headteacher (Primary Level)

Supports the Headteacher in administration and student affairs at the Primary Level.

Headmistress (Kindergarten)

Leads early childhood education programs, nurturing creativity and foundational learning skills.

Senior Teachers & Staff

Experienced educators and administrative staff who maintain academic excellence and school operations.
